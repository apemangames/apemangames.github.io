Everyone gets the same secret word, except one person—the Liar! Take turns describing your word with a single word. Then, guess who the Liar is. The game ends after 3 rounds or if the Liar is unanimously caught.

Players

  • +1 Point for correct guesses
  • +3 Points for voting the Liar
  • -1 Point for incorrect guesses
  • -2 Points for incorrect votes

The Liar

  • +1 Point when others guess wrong
  • +3 Points for surviving a round
